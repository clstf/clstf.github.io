PERCOLATORS

CAFETIERES

Open the percolator;
Add water up untill the the valve level;
Place the coffee holder part;
Fill it with ground coffee;
Screw the top part;
Place on top of the hob/stove;
Wait for it to boil;
Serve.

Open the cafetiere;
Pour ground coffee to taste;
Add hot (not boiling) water;
Mix with a spoon;
Insert plunger;
Push it down a bit and let it settle;
Push the rest down until the plunger reaches the coffee level;
Serve.
